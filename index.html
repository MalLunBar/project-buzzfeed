Here are five shocking DNA test results that people have discovered!

Switched at Birth

A man took a DNA test for fun and found out he wasn't biologically related to his parents. After investigating, he learned he was accidentally switched at birth in the hospital.

Secret Sibling

A woman took a DNA test and found a half-brother she never knew existed. Turns out, her father had an affair and kept it a secret for decades.

Family's Dark Past

A woman researching her family tree found out her grandfather wasn't who they thought—he had changed his name to hide his criminal past, which involved fleeing another country.

Married to a Cousin

A couple took DNA tests out of curiosity and were horrified to discover they were actually cousins. They had been married for years and had no idea they were related.

Dad Wasn't Really Dad

A man in his 40s gifted his whole family DNA test kits for Christmas. The results revealed that his father wasn't his biological dad, his mother had a brief relationship with someone else.
